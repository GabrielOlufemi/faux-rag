1. User uploads PDF
   ↓
2. FastAPI receives file
   ↓
3. Extract text (PyPDF2)
   ↓
4. Split into chunks (RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter)
   ↓
5. Generate embeddings (Sentence Transformers)
   ↓
6. Store in Pinecone with metadata
   ↓
7. User asks question
   ↓
8. Convert question to embedding
   ↓
9. Search Pinecone for relevant chunks
   ↓
10. Send chunks + question to Gemini
    ↓
11. Return Gemini's response to user
